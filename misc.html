Email management during the interim period between graduating from RPI and joining Siemens

According to RPI's policy, all accounts, including Email address with RPI domains, will expire after my graduation. I am applying the following rules to manage incoming mails:
1) Starting May 1st, all RPI mails will be forwarded to my personal email address. I will still reply with RPI address until the graduation date (May 18th, 2019) and will use my personal email address afterwards.
2) Approximately 6 weeks after graudation date, the RPI address will expire and I am not able to reply with the address, but the autoforwarding will persist for additional 6 months.
3) After 6 months (the end of 2019), mails sent to RPI address will be bounced.
4) After officially starting in Siemens (in later June or July), I will use the company's email address for work and research affairs, however, I will NOT reroute mails sent to RPI address to the company's inbox, and I will still reply with my personal email address to these RPI mails unless instructed.
5) During the interim period, I am unsubscribing some services bounded to my RPI address. I will proceed with caution so that it will no affect anything, including shared codes and services.
